Recipe for 'Kaiserschmarrn'

Ingredients:
100 grams Flour
3 Eggs
100 milliliters Milk
1 pinch Salt
2 tablespoons Sugar
50 grams Raisins 
30 grams Butter 
Icing Sugar

Instructions: 
Separate eggs.
Mix flour, egg yolks, salt and milk to smooth dough.
Whip egg whites gradually adding sugar. 
Fold into dough. Stir in raisins. 
Melt butter in pan, add dough. Let it thicken on low heat.
Tear apart with forks, flip pieces over.
Bake until golden brown.
Sprinkle with icing sugar.
