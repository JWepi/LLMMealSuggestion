Recipe for 'Watermelon Feta Salad'

Ingredients:

- 1 small watermelon
- 200g feta cheese
- 2 handfuls fresh mint leaves
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Remove seeds from watermelon.
2. Cut into bite-sized cubes.
3. Crumble feta cheese.
4. Tear mint leaves by hand.
5. Combine watermelon, feta cheese and mint in a bowl.
6. Drizzle olive oil over top of ingredients in the bowl.
7. Season with salt and pepper as desired.
8. Toss gently until mixed completely.
9. Serve immediately or chill before serving for extra refreshment.
