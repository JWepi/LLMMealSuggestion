Recipe for 'Chicken Paprikash'

Ingredients:
1 kilogram chicken 
2 onions
2 tablespoons oil
3 tablespoons paprika powder
400 grams canned tomatoes
300 milliliters chicken broth
200 milliliters sour cream
Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Chop onions.
2. Heat oil in pan.
3. Add onions, stir until golden.
4. Add chicken, cook till browned.
5. Sprinkle paprika, mix well.
6. Pour tomatoes and broth into pan, mix.
7. Cover pan, simmer for 20 minutes.
8. Stir in sour cream before serving.
9.Sprinkle salt as needed.
