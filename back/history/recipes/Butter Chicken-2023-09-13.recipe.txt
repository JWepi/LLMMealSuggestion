Recipe for 'Butter Chicken'

Ingredients:
600 grams Chicken
200 grams Butter
400 grams Tomatoes
200 milliliters Cream 
100 grams Onion
2 Green Chillies 
20 grams Garlic 
20 grams Ginger 
5 grams Cumin Seeds
5 Cardamom Pods 
1 teaspoon Turmeric Powder 
1 teaspoon Red Chili Powder  
2 teaspoons Coriander Powder  
Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken with salt, red chili powder and turmeric.
2. Heat half butter in pan.
3. Add marinated chicken, cook till done.
4. Remove chicken, keep aside.
5. In same pan, add remaining butter.
6. Add cumin seeds, cardamom pods to pan.
7. Next, add chopped onion, green chillies, garlic and ginger to pan.
8. Cook until onions golden brown.
9. Add tomatoes and coriander powder to the mix.
10.Cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.
11.Blend mixture into smooth paste.
12.Return paste to the pan on low heat .
13.Add cooked chicken pieces back into the creamy gravy .
14.Pour cream over it all for added richness .
15.Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving .
