Recipe for 'Kartoffelsuppe'

Ingredients:
1 kilogram potatoes
2 liters vegetable broth
2 onions
3 cloves garlic
200 grams leek
200 grams carrots 
200 grams celeriac 
Salt to taste 
Pepper to taste 
Chopped parsley

Instructions:

1. Peel and cube potatoes.
2. Chop onions, garlic, leek, carrots, and celeriac.
3. Boil vegetables in broth until tender.
4. Blend soup until smooth.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
