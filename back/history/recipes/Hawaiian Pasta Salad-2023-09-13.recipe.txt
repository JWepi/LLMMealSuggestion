Recipe for 'Hawaiian Pasta Salad'

Ingredients:
- 340 grams pasta
- 1 pineapple, chopped 
- 3 bell peppers (red, yellow, green), diced
- 500 grams ham, diced
- 150 grams cheddar cheese, cubed
- 250 milliliters mayonnaise 
- 125 milliliters Greek yogurt 
- Pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta.
2. Drain and cool.
3. In large bowl combine pasta, pineapple, bell peppers, ham and cheese.
4. In another bowl mix mayonnaise and Greek yogurt.
5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
6. Pour dressing over pasta mixture.
7. Toss until well combined.
8. Chill before serving.
