Recipe for 'Koshari'

Ingredients:
- 200 grams lentils
- 2 liters water
- 400 grams rice
- 200 grams macaroni 
- 50 milliliters olive oil
- 2 onions, chopped 
- Salt to taste 
- Black pepper to taste 
- Tomato sauce

Instructions:
1. Cook lentils in water until tender.
2. Cook rice and macaroni separately.
3. Heat oil in pan, add onions, saut√© until browned.
4. Mix lentils, rice, macaroni together.
5. Add salt and black pepper.
6. Pour tomato sauce on top before serving.

Note: Convert quantities as needed depending on servings required or dietary needs/preferences.
