Recipe for 'Psari Plaki'

Ingredients:
1 Kg Fish fillet
2 Onions
4 Garlic cloves
4 Tomatoes 
2 Green peppers 
50 g Parsley 
75 ml Olive oil 
125 ml White wine 
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
2. Slice onions, garlic, tomatoes, and peppers.
3. Place fish in baking dish.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Layer sliced vegetables over fish.
6. Drizzle olive oil and wine on top.
7. Bake for 40 minutes or until fish is cooked through.
8. Garnish with parsley before serving.
