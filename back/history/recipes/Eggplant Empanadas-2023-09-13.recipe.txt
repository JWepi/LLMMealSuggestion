Recipe for 'Eggplant Empanadas'

Ingredients:
- 2 eggplants
- 100 grams of mozzarella cheese
- 50 grams of parmesan cheese
- 1 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- Salt to taste 
- Pepper to taste 
- Olive oil, as needed 
- Dough for empanadas, ready-made

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
2. Chop eggplant, onion and garlic.
3. Place on baking sheet, drizzle with oil.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Bake until soft and golden brown.
6. Remove from oven, let cool slightly.
7. Mix cheeses together in bowl.
8. Add cooled vegetables to cheese mix.
9. Fill empanada dough circles with vegetable-cheese mixture.
10. Fold dough over filling, crimp edges with fork to seal.
11.Reduce oven temperature to 180°C,
12.Bake empanadas until golden brown and crispy.
