Recipe for 'Louisiana Cajun Pasta'

Ingredients:
- 500 grams pasta
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 500 grams chicken breast
- 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
- 225 grams smoked sausage 
- 2 cloves garlic 
- 400 ml chicken broth 
- 300 ml heavy cream 
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste 
- Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta, set aside.
2. Heat oil in pan.
3. Cut chicken breast into chunks, season with Cajun seasoning.
4. Cook chicken and sausage in pan until browned.
5. Add minced garlic, stir briefly.
6. Pour in chicken broth and heavy cream, simmer till slightly reduced.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Add cooked pasta into the pan, mix well.
9. Garnish with parsley before serving.

Note: Adjust spice level as per preference using Cajun seasoning or red pepper flakes if desired
