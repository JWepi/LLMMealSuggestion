Recipe for 'Mediterranean Tuna Salad Wrap'

Ingredients:
- 1 can tuna
- 1/2 cup chickpeas
- 1/4 cup red onion
- 1/4 cup cucumber
- 1/4 cup olives
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Wrap or pita bread

Instructions:
1. Drain and flake tuna.
2. Chop onion, cucumber, and olives.
3. In a bowl, combine tuna, chickpeas, onion, cucumber, olives.
4. Add lemon juice, salt and pepper; mix well.
5. Place the mixture on wrap or pita bread.
6. Fold and enjoy!
