Recipe for 'Labskaus'

Ingredients:
500g corned beef
500g potatoes
2 onions 
250ml beef broth 
4 pickles 
50ml pickle juice 
1 tsp mustard 
2 tbsp vegetable oil
Salt and pepper to taste
4 pickled beetroot slices
4 fried eggs

Instructions:

1. Cube corned beef, potatoes, onions, and pickles.
2. Heat oil in pan.
3. Add onions, saut√© until translucent.
4. Mix in corned beef and potatoes.
5. Pour in broth, simmer for 20 minutes.
6. Stir in pickle cubes and juice, cook 10 more minutes.
7. Mash mixture into coarse consistency.
8. Season with mustard, salt, pepper.
9. Serve with beetroot slice on top and fried egg over it all.

Enjoy your Labskaus!
