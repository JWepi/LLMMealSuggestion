Recipe for 'Carnitas Tacos'

Ingredients:
- 1 kilogram pork shoulder
- 1 liter water
- 200 grams white onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced 
- 2 oranges, juiced
- 2 limes, juiced
- Salt to taste 
- Corn tortillas 
- Fresh cilantro for garnish 
- Salsa of choice

Instructions:

1. Cut pork into chunks.
2. In large pot, add pork and water.
3. Add onion, garlic, orange juice, lime juice and salt.
4. Cook on medium heat for about two hours until tender.
5. Shred meat with fork and remove from liquid.
6. Heat corn tortillas in pan until warm.
7. Place shredded meat onto tortilla.
8. Garnish with cilantro and salsa before serving.

Enjoy your Carnitas Tacos!
