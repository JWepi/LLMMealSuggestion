<h3>Recipe for '**New York-style Pizza**'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>* 500g bread flour
* 2g active dry yeast
* 2g sugar
* 2g salt
* 300ml water
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 200g canned crushed tomatoes
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1 tsp dried basil
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
* Handful of fresh basil leaves</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>1. In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
2. Add water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
3. Knead dough on a floured surface for 5-10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise for 1-2 hours, until doubled in size.
5. Preheat oven to 250Â°C.
6. Roll out dough on a floured surface to a thickness of about 0.5cm.
7. Transfer dough to a greased or parchment-lined baking sheet.
8. In a small bowl, mix together crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, and minced garlic.
9. Spread tomato mixture evenly over dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
10. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over tomato mixture.
11. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
12. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
13. Slice and serve. Enjoy!</span>
