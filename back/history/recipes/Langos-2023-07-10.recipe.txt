Recipe for 'Langos'

Ingredients:
500 grams flour
30 grams fresh yeast
250 milliliters water
1 teaspoon salt
2 cloves garlic
200 milliliters vegetable oil

Instructions:
Dissolve yeast in warm water.
Mix flour and salt.
Add yeast mixture to dry ingredients.
Knead dough, let rise for 1 hour.
Divide into 8 parts, flatten each piece. 
Heat oil in pan.
Fry dough pieces until golden brown.
Rub with cut garlic clove.
