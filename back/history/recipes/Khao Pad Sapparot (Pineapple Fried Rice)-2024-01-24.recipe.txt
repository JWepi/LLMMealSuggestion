<h3>Recipe for 'Khao Pad Sapparot (Pineapple Fried Rice)'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
150 grams Jasmine rice
200 grams Pineapple chunks
50 grams Sweet corn kernels 
75 grams Chicken bites
30 grams Cashew nuts  
15 ml Vegetable oil   
10 ml Soy sauce   
5 g Spring onions 
5 g Curry powder  
2 Thai bird eye chili, chopped</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Prepare the jasmine rice. Set aside.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Heat vegetable oil in a pan.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Add chicken bites. Stir fry until cooked through.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Mix in curry powder and Thai bird eye chili.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Add pineapple chunks and sweet corn. </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Stir in cooked Jasmine rice and mix well.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Pour soy sauce into mixture.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Toss cashew nuts on top of the dish.  </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Garnish with spring onions before serving warm.
</span>
