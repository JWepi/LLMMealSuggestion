Recipe for 'Ropa Vieja Canaria'

Ingredients:
500g beef
1 onion
1 green pepper
2 garlic cloves
300g tomatoes
50ml white wine
1 bay leaf

Instructions:
1. Boil beef until tender.
2. Shred meat and set aside.
3. Saut√© onion, green pepper, garlic.
4. Add tomatoes, cook for 5 mins.
5. Add shredded beef, white wine, bay leaf.
6. Cook on low heat for 30 mins.
7. Serve with rice or potatoes.
