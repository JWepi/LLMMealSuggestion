Recipe for 'Greek Lemon Rice Soup (Avgolemono)'

Ingredients:
- 1 cup rice
- 6 cups water or chicken broth
- 2 lemons, juiced
- 3 eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook rice in water or chicken broth until tender.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together lemon juice and eggs.
3. Slowly add ladlefuls of hot broth to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
4. Pour the egg mixture back into the pot with the rice.
5. Heat gently on low heat until slightly thickened.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve immediately.
