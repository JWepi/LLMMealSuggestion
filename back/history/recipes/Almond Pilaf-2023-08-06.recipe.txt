Recipe for 'Almond Pilaf'

Ingredients:
- 225 grams Basmati rice
- 60 ml olive oil
- 75 grams sliced almonds
- 600 ml vegetable broth
- Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. Rinse the rice.
2. Heat oil in a pan.
3. Add almonds, cook until golden brown.
4. Remove half of the almonds for garnishing.
5. Add rice to remaining almonds and oil, stir well.
6. Pour vegetable broth into pan, add salt.
7. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
8. Fluff with fork before serving and garnish with reserved almonds.
