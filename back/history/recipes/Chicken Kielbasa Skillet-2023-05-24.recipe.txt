Recipe for 'Chicken Kielbasa Skillet'

Ingredients:
500 g chicken kielbasa
1 onion
2 garlic cloves
400 g canned diced tomatoes
200 g green beans
200 g mushrooms
1 bell pepper
2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:
1. Chop onion, garlic, bell pepper, and mushrooms.
2. Slice chicken kielbasa.
3. Heat olive oil in skillet.
4. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until softened.
5. Add chicken kielbasa, cook for 5 minutes.
6. Stir in tomatoes, green beans, mushrooms, and bell pepper.
7. Cook until vegetables are tender and kielbasa is cooked through.

Serve with rice or pasta if desired.
