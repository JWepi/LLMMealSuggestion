<h3>Recipe for 'Hortobágyi Palacsinta'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>150 grams veal
1 onion
3 bell peppers
1 tomato 
100 grams flour
2 eggs
500 milliliters milk 
50 grams butter 
Salt and pepper to taste </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions: </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Chop onion, bell peppers, tomato.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Sauté in pan until tender. </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Add veal, continue cooking. </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Whisk flour, eggs, milk until smooth.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Heat butter in pan. </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Pour batter into pan.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Cook until browned. </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Fill with veal mixture.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Serve warm.</span>
