Recipe for 'Scalloped Potatoes'

Ingredients:
- 2 kg potatoes
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 tablespoon salt
- Half teaspoon pepper 
- 1 litre milk 
- 4 tablespoons butter 
- 200 grams all-purpose flour
- Half litre heavy cream 
- 200 grams shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees Celsius.
2. Peel, slice potatoes.
3. Mince garlic.
4. In dish, combine potatoes, garlic, salt, pepper.
5. Heat saucepan over medium heat.
6. Add butter till melted.
7. Stir in flour until smooth texture.
8. Gradually whisk in milk and heavy cream till mixture thickens.
9. Remove from heat; stir in cheese until melted and mixed well.
10. Pour mixture over potato mix in dish evenly.
11. Bake for hour or until golden brown on top and potatoes are tender when pierced with fork.

Serve hot after few minutes of cooling down after baking completion.
