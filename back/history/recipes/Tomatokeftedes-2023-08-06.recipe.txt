Recipe for 'Tomatokeftedes'

Ingredients:
- 5 ripe tomatoes
- 1 large red onion
- 2 cloves garlic 
- 250g flour 
- 15g fresh mint 
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste
- Sunflower oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Grate tomatoes.
2. Mince onion and garlic.
3. Combine all ingredients except oil.
4. Mix until dough forms.
5. Shape into patties.
6. Heat sunflower oil in pan over medium heat.
7. Fry patties until golden brown on each side, about 4 minutes per side.
8. Drain on paper towels before serving.
