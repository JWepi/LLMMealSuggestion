<h3>Recipe for 'Bulgogi Rice Bowl'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
500 grams beef tenderloin
250 milliliters soy sauce
100 grams brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced 
1 onion
1 carrot
200 grams spinach 
200 grams mushrooms  
2 cups cooked rice</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:
1. Slice beef thinly.
2. Mix soy sauce, brown sugar, and garlic to make marinade.
3. Marinate beef for 30 minutes.
4. Sauté onions, carrots in pan.
5. Add marinated beef, cook until done.
6. Sauté mushrooms and spinach separately.
7. Prepare bowl with cooked rice at bottom.
8. Top with sautéed veggies and cooked beef.
9. Serve hot.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Note: Adjust ingredients to taste preference if needed</span>
