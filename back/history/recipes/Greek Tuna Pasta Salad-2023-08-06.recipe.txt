Recipe for 'Greek Tuna Pasta Salad'

Ingredients:
- 400 grams pasta
- 2 cans tuna, drained
- 100 grams black olives, pitted and sliced
- 200 grams cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cucumber, diced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 150 grams feta cheese, crumbled 
- Juice of one lemon 
- Olive oil as needed 
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta. Drain.
2. Mix tuna with pasta in large bowl.
3. Add olives, tomatoes, cucumber and onion.
4. Top with feta cheese.
5. Squeeze lemon juice over salad.
6. Drizzle olive oil over salad.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Toss everything together gently until well combined.

Serve chilled or at room temperature as preferred.
