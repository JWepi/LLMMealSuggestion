<h3>Recipe for 'Thai Green Curry'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
- 500g chicken 
- 400ml coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons green curry paste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 10 Thai basil leaves 
- 2 kaffir lime leaves   
- fish sauce to taste
- sugar to taste
 </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:
Heat oil.     
Add curry paste, stir for two minutes.
Add chicken, cook until white.
Pour in coconut milk.      
Simmer for ten minutes.
Add lime and basil leaves.      
Season with fish sauce and sugar.
Serve hot with rice.</span>
