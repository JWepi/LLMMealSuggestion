Recipe for 'Italian Stuffed Chicken Breast'

Ingredients:
4 chicken breasts
100 grams spinach 
50 grams sun-dried tomatoes
100 grams mozzarella cheese
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon garlic powder 
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
Flatten chicken breasts.
Cook spinach.
Chop sun-dried tomatoes, mozzarella. 
Mix spinach, tomatoes, mozzarella. 
Add salt, pepper.  
Stuff mix into chicken breasts.
Drizzle with oil.
Sprinkle with garlic powder.
Bake at 200Â°C for 25 minutes.
