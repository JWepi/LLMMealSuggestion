Recipe for 'Eggplant Parmigiana'

Eggplants:
2 medium eggplants
Salt

Breadcrumbs mixture:
150 g breadcrumbs
50 g grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp dried oregano

Batter:
100 g flour
200 ml water

Tomato sauce:
500 ml tomato passata
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Filling:
300 g mozzarella cheese, sliced
Handful of fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

1. Slice eggplants and salt them.
2. Mix breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, and oregano.
3. Prepare batter by mixing flour and water.
4. Sauté garlic in olive oil for 1 minute.
5. Add tomato passata, salt and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes.

Assembly:

6. Dip eggplant slices in batter, coat with breadcrumb mixture.
7. Fry breaded eggplant slices until golden brown; drain on paper towels.
8. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce in baking dish.
9. Add a layer of fried eggplant slices; top with more sauce, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves.
10. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used up; finish with a layer of sauce and mozzarella on top.

11. Bake at 200°C (390°F) for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown on top.

12.Allow to rest for a few minutes before serving
