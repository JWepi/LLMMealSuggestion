Recipe for 'Baked Idaho Potato'

Ingredients:
- 1 Idaho potato (around 200g)
- Sea salt (2g)
- Olive oil (15ml)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celsius.
2. Wash and dry the potato.
3. Prick several times with a fork.
4. Coat lightly in olive oil.
5. Sprinkle sea salt over the entire surface of the potato.
6. Place directly on middle rack of preheated oven.
7. Bake for around 60 minutes or until easily pierced with a fork.
8. Let cool for five minutes before serving.

Note: Can be served plain or with toppings of your choice such as sour cream, chives, cheese, etc.
