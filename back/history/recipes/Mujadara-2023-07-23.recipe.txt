Recipe for 'Mujadara'

Ingredients:
200 grams green lentils
120 ml olive oil
2 onions 
300 grams long-grain rice
1 teaspoon cumin 
1/2 teaspoon turmeric 
1 teaspoon allspice 
1 litre water 
Salt to taste

Instructions:
Rinse lentils.
Boil over medium heat until tender.
Drain, set aside.
Chop onions.
Heat half of the olive oil in pan.
Add onions, cook until browned.
Reserve half of the fried onions for garnish.
Add rice to remaining onions in pan. 
Stir well.
Add cooked lentils back into pan with rice and onions.  
Mix in spices.
Pour in water, add salt.
Bring to a boil.

Cover pot and simmer on low heat until water is absorbed and rice is cooked.

Serve hot, garnished with reserved fried onions.
