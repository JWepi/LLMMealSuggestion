<h3>Recipe for 'Kaiserschmarrn'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
140 grams Flour
4 Eggs
50 milliliters Milk
40 grams Sugar
1 pinch Salt
40 grams Raisins
2 tablespoons Rum 
20 grams Butter </span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:
1. Separate eggs.
2. Mix yolks, flour, milk, sugar and salt.
3. Soak raisins in rum.
4. Beat egg whites until stiff.
5. Fold raisins and egg whites into batter.
6. Add butter to heated pan.
7. Pour batter in pan, cook on low heat till golden brown underneath.
8. Flip, tear into pieces with fork while frying other side till golden brown too.
9. Sprinkle sugar over finished Kaiserschmarrn before serving.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Note: Serve hot with powdered sugar and apple sauce or berry compote if desired.</span>
