<h3>Recipe for 'Salsa Verde Chicken Casserole'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
- 500 grams chicken breasts
- 1 onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 24 tortillas
- 475 milliliters salsa verde 
- 240 milliliters sour cream 
- 300 grams shredded Monterey Jack cheese 
- Salt</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:
1. Cook chicken.
2. Shred cooked chicken.
3. Chop onion and garlic.
4. Sauté onion and garlic.
5. Mix chicken, sautéed onions, and garlic with salsa verde and sour cream.
6. Layer half the tortillas in baking dish.
7. Add half of chicken mixture on top of tortillas.
8. Sprinkle half of cheese over mixture.
9. Repeat layers with remaining ingredients.
10. Bake at 190°C for about 25 minutes until bubbly & browned on top.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Enjoy your Salsa Verde Chicken Casserole!</span>
