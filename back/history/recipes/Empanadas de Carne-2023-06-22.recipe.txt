Recipe for 'Empanadas de Carne'

Ingredients:
300g ground beef
2 onions
2 bell peppers
50g green olives
3 hard-boiled eggs
500g flour
100g butter
1 egg

Instructions:
1. Brown ground beef in a pan.
2. Add diced onions and bell peppers, cook until softened.
3. Stir in chopped olives and hard-boiled eggs, set aside to cool.
4. Mix flour, butter, 1 egg, and water to form dough.
5. Roll out dough and cut into circles.
6. Place filling onto dough circles.
7. Fold over and seal edges with a fork.
8. Bake at 200Â°C for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
