Recipe for 'Mexican Chocolate Cake'

Ingredients:
- 200 grams dark chocolate
- 225 grams unsalted butter
- 325 grams sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 350 milliliters strong coffee 
- 250 grams all-purpose flour
- 50 grams cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking powder 
- Half a teaspoon of cinnamon 
- Pinch of chili pepper

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
2. Melt chocolate and butter.
3. Add sugar, mix until dissolved.
4. Add eggs, one by one.
5. Pour in coffee, stir well.
6. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, cinnamon and chili pepper in separate bowl.
7. Gradually add dry ingredients into wet mixture.
8. Pour batter into cake tin.
9. Bake for about an hour or until toothpick comes out clean.
10. Let cool before serving.

Enjoy your Mexican Chocolate Cake!
