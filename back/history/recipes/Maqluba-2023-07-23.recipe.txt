Recipe for 'Maqluba'

Ingredients:
1 kg chicken
500 g Basmati rice
500 g eggplant
3 carrots
2 potatoes
2 onions
5 cloves garlic 
4 tbsp olive oil
2 tsp turmeric powder 
1 tsp allspice 
Salt to taste  
Pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cut vegetables into slices.
2. Fry each vegetable in oil, then set aside.
3. Season chicken with spices.
4. Fry until golden brown.
4. Remove chicken from pan, leave oil behind.
5. Layer vegetables in pan: potatoes, carrots, eggplant.
6. Add fried chicken on top of vegetables.
7. Rinse and drain rice, add onto the top layer of chicken in pot.
8. Pour enough water over layers to cover them by about 2 cm.
9. Bring it to boil then reduce heat and simmer for 40-50 minutes.
10. Let it rest for 10 minutes before flipping over a serving platter (maqluba).
11.Serve warm with yogurt or salad on side if desired.

Note: Maqluba is an upside-down Middle Eastern rice dish that includes meat and fried vegetables cooked in one pot and flipped over before serving; hence its name "Maqluba" which translates literally as "upside-down".
