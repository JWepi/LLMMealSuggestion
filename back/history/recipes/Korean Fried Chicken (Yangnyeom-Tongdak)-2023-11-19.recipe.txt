<h3>Recipe for 'Korean Fried Chicken (Yangnyeom-Tongdak)'</h3><img src='icons/download.png' class='downloadRecipe'/>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Ingredients:
1 kilogram chicken wings
120 grams potato starch
2 liters vegetable oil
5 milliliters rice vinegar 
60 milliliters gochujang (Korean chili paste)
30 grams honey
15 grams brown sugar 
15 grams soy sauce 
1 garlic clove, minced</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Instructions:
1. Coat chicken in potato starch.
2. Heat vegetable oil to 175Â°C.
3. Fry chicken for 10 minutes.
4. Drain and cool for 5 minutes.
5. Refry chicken for another 10 minutes.
6. Mix rice vinegar, gochujang, honey, brown sugar, soy sauce and garlic in a bowl.
7. Toss fried chicken in sauce until evenly coated.</span>

<span class='finalDishStepArea'>Enjoy your Korean Fried Chicken (Yangnyeom-Tongdak)!</span>
