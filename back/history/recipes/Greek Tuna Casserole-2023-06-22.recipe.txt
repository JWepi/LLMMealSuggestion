Greek Tuna Casserole

Ingredients:
200g pasta
1 can tuna
150g feta cheese
100g cherry tomatoes
50g black olives
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp oregano

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta.
2. Drain tuna.
3. Halve tomatoes.
4. Slice olives.
5. Crumble feta cheese.

6. In a casserole dish, combine all ingredients and mix well.
7. Bake at 180Â°C for 20 minutes or until heated through.
